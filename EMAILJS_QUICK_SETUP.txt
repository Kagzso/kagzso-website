# Quick Fix: EmailJS Setup (3-Minute Version)

**Don't want to read the full guide? Here's the fastest path:**

## Step 1: Get Your Keys (2 minutes)
1. Go to https://www.emailjs.com/
2. Sign up free (use your Gmail: kagzso.in@gmail.com)
3. Verify email
4. Go to **Account** â†’ **API Keys**
5. Copy your **Public Key** (e.g., `abc123xyz456...`)

## Step 2: Connect Email Service (1 minute)
1. Still in EmailJS dashboard
2. Go to **Email Services**
3. Click **Add Service**
4. Choose **Gmail**
5. Click **Connect with Gmail** and authorize
6. Copy the **Service ID** (e.g., `gmail_service`)

## Step 3: Create Template (3 minutes)
1. Go to **Email Templates**
2. Click **Create New Template**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** `contact_form`
   - **Service:** `gmail_service` (from step 2)
   - **To Email:** `{{to_email}}`
   - **From:** `{{from_email}}`
4. Paste this in Content:
```
Name: {{from_name}}
Email: {{from_email}}
Phone: {{phone}}
Business: {{business_type}}

Message:
{{message}}
```
5. Click **Save**
6. Copy the **Template ID** (should be `contact_form`)

## Step 4: Update Your Code (30 seconds)

Open `index.html` and find these two lines (around line 1224):

**Line 1) Find this:**
```javascript
emailjs.init('YOUR_EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY');
```

**Replace with:**
```javascript
emailjs.init('paste-your-public-key-here');
```

**Line 2) Find this:**
```javascript
emailjs.send('SERVICE_ID', 'TEMPLATE_ID', {
```

**Replace with:**
```javascript
emailjs.send('gmail_service', 'contact_form', {
```

## Step 5: Test (1 minute)
1. Refresh your website
2. Fill in the form
3. Click "Get Free Automation Assessment"
4. Check your email for the submission
